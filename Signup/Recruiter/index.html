University Registration Notice

To maintain the accuracy and authenticity of university profiles on Unisuniverse.com, we follow a secure and invitation-based registration process.

Here’s how it works:
When a recruiter wishes to create or access their university page, our system first checks whether the university already exists in our database.

• If the university already exists: a login ID and password are sent to the university’s official email address (e.g., info@university.edu). The recruiter can then use these credentials to log in and begin updating the existing page.


• If the university does not exist: a unique registration link is sent to the same official email address. The recruiter can use that link to start the process of creating the university profile from scratch.


In both cases, all communication is made through the verified institutional email to ensure the legitimacy of the request and avoid unauthorized page creation.

To keep your institution informed, we’ve also sent a notification to the university’s info email containing your name and request details, so they are aware of your interest in managing the university’s page.

If you agree with this process, please complete the form below to submit your university and contact details. Our team will verify the information and send the appropriate registration credentials or invitation link to your institution.
