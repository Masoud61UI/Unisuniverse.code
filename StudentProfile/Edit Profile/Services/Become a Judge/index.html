Become a Judge

Service Introduction

What this is

Join Unisuniverse.com as an independent dispute judge and help resolve user conflicts (e.g., quality of work, late delivery, referencing, plagiarism).

How you earn

Based on your expertise, experience, and keywords, the system will match you to cases. For every case you accept and decide, you receive a case fee (complex cases may include an additional fee).

What we need from you

Select your broad expertise areas and provide specific keywords (methods, tools, standards, topics). These keywords power our matching algorithm.

Neutrality & ethics

Judges must remain impartial, declare conflicts of interest, and accept the Code of Conduct. All verdicts are reasoned and recorded in the case timeline.
